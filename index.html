    I am a Master's student in Electrical Engineering at Universität Stuttgart, currently completing my thesis at the Institut für Automatisierungstechnik und Softwaresysteme (IAS), focusing on soft labeling in image classifiers. With over two years of diverse experience in machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, I have contributed to impactful projects in autonomous systems and industrial applications. During my internship at Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence, I evaluated advanced vision transformers through data manipulation in real-world scenarios, while at Daimler Truck AG, I developed custom scripts and VBA integrations to optimize simulation tools. At Fraunhofer IPA, I have enhanced transport monitoring systems and currently focus on object detection in computer vision projects. Proficient in Python, C++, and Matlab, with expertise in PyTorch and TensorFlow, I have applied my skills to Li-ion battery modeling, driverless systems, and robustness evaluation in machine learning, presenting findings on adversarial distance estimation at IMECE 2024.
